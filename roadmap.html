Our Roadmap

Faythe's main focus is the decentralization, security and freedom of our future. We are technology driven, with privacy and security being two of our main goals. With operations on 5 continents Faythe has a global vision that will disrupt the traditional financial system.

Faythe net

Release of the Faythe test network to academics, researchers and developers (invite only)

With the release of Faythe Alpha Net, the Faythe project entered a phase of decentralized research and development that will help shape up the Faythe Protocol and create all the infrastructure required by Faythe I/O.

Trent ERC20

Creation of the Trent ethereum based token and start of TTP token distribution

Trent TTP tokens were issued as an Ethereum based ERC20, managing to keep contribution to the Faythe Project both secure and anonymous. Tokens are distributed in 3 phases: Prime Movers, Brain Gain and Streakings. All TTP ERC20 tokens will be converted to Trent Coins after Main Net launch.

Faythe ERC20

Creation of the Faythe ethereum based token of FYE token distribution

Faythe FYE tokens were issued as an Ethereum based ERC20. FYE tokens were distributed as bonus in the first 2 distribution phases: Prime Movers and Brain Gain. FYE tokens are also available as a bonus in return for TTP ERC20 donations made to the dev team. All FYE ERC20 tokens will be converted to Faythe Coins after Faythe EVO2 launch.

Prime Movers

PRE-ICO open to the public, 1000 ETH raised

PRE-ICO was open to the public offering Trent TTP ERC20 tokens at a rate of 1000 TTP for 1 ETH. 1,000,000 TTP tokens were distributed to contributors. Prime Movers contributors also received an allocation of 21,000 FYE tokens as a plot bonus.

Brain Gain

ICO open to the public

ICO took place in June 2018 with all 100,628,393 TTP ERC20 tokens being allocated for a total contribution of 144,097 ETH in just 441 hours. This also set a new value for 1 TTP token compared to ETH, at a rate of 698 ETH for 1 TTP.

Streakings

Quota based distribution in public crowd sales

A total of 311 Streaking, each covering a period of 21 hours will make sure that 100% of the TTP tokens will get distributed with full security and fairness to all contributors. Tokens are available to plot contributors and can be claimed at the end of each Streaking period.

Trent Paper

Release of the Trent White Paper presenting the evolution and integration of the Main Net

The Trent Paper will present the research, innovation, development and token to coin swap information related to the release of the Trent 0.1.0 Main Net. Trent paper will present the features, road map and stages of the token to Main Net to Trent EVO2 Net evolution.

Trent 0.1.0

Release of the first public version the Trent Main Net

Official release of the first public version Trent Main Net. All TTP ERC20 ethereum based tokens will be swappable for Trent Coins and Trent Main Net will be integrated with exchanges and third party service providers.

Faythe Logic Map

Release of the Faythe Tech Paper and Logic Map

The Faythe Tech Paper and Faythe Logic Map will present all research and information conducted on the Faythe Alpha Net. This will be the first public introduction of the Faythe Protocol, Faythe I/O and all information related to third party Faythe implementation requirements.

Trent EVO2

Trent EVO2 Dev gets released to the public with full Faythe I/O implementation

The second iteration of the Trent Blockchain will bring 5th generation functionality to the Trent EVO2 Dev, while also supporting full inputs and outputs in order to prepare for Faythe EVO2 deployment and integration.

Faythe EVO2

End of Faythe Alpha Net test period. Faythe EVO2 resource, dapp and blockchain bridge release

Faythe EVO2 will offer full public access to the Faythe protocols. Developers will be able to publish and run decentralized apps that will full multi crypto currency support.
